Tyler Yaish

Musician/Sound Engineer/Writer

Music has always been a huge part of my life.

Writing music is something that is very important to me. I tend to spend a lot of time working on music and I like to keep my lyrics personal. I started making music a few years ago just for fun, writing random rhymes that popped up in my head and playing melodies on the piano. Over time, my passion for music grew and it’s now what I work on for hours each day.

If I didn’t constantly surround myself with music, I’d have a harder time trying to express myself because honestly, if there’s ever something I need to get off my chest or write down, I’ll put it into the form of lyrics. It took a lot of trial and error at first to figure out what I wanted to do with music, but it didn’t take me too long to try and do something with it. I put together a few songs and shortly after, released them everywhere for people to listen to. Doing this was nerve wracking for me because I had never put music out for others to listen to, but then I started to get a lot of positive feedback from the songs. This felt really cool for me because I didn’t expect people to enjoy my random creativity. My songs have even gotten to the point where they’ve helped inspire some of my peers to make their own music. It is something I definitely plan on pursuing as a side job in the future, or even as a hobby for the rest of my life.
